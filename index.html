About Us

SMART HEALTH DOO is an innovative company founded at the Institute for Cardiovascular Diseases "Dedinje". We specialize in developing and commercializing medical equipment and services through the application of the latest 3D technologies.

Our mission is to accelerate the implementation of new diagnostic and therapeutic approaches, improve prevention and medical personnel education through advanced 3D medical models and products.

Research

Mission

We develop and transfer innovations in cardiovascular medicine into real clinical practice through clinically grounded research, development of health technologies and digital solutions. Our mission is to accelerate the implementation of new diagnostic and therapeutic approaches, improve prevention and rehabilitation.

Vision

To become a leading regional partner for the development and commercialization of health technologies in cardiovascular medicine. We aim to market innovations with global potential from Serbia and strengthen scientific and technological capacity through Horizon Europe and international programs.

Research & Development

Conducts research projects, develops prototypes, and tests new technologies

Smart Health Village

The Smart Health Village concept represents a modern approach to strengthening healthcare capacities, with a focus on innovative rehabilitation, post-hospital care, and preventive health—primarily for cardiovascular patients. Building on European Union principles for developing smart and sustainable areas, this model integrates improved healthcare services, digital technologies, environmental sustainability, and social inclusion into a coherent framework aimed at enhancing the well-being of our population.

Rather than relying solely on traditional rehabilitation services, the Smart Health Village creates new opportunities through:

  • Innovative models of cardiovascular rehabilitation
  • Community-based post-hospital care
  • Primary and secondary prevention
  • Health monitoring through advanced digital technologies (including the use of artificial intelligence)
  • Education for patients and the wider community

This model contributes to a significant reduction of pressure on the healthcare system, decreases the need for re-hospitalisation, improves patient recovery in a setting close to home, and at the same time supports the development of Serbia's health policy.

Medical Waste Management

Development and implementation of innovative, safe, and environmentally friendly solutions for the collection, treatment, and disposal of medical waste, with a particular focus on hazardous medical waste from healthcare facilities and rehabilitation centres, in line with the highest European and national standards.

National and International Innovation and Research Projects
