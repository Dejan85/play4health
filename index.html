About Us

SMART HEALTH DOO is an innovative company founded at the Institute for Cardiovascular Diseases "Dedinje". We specialize in developing and commercializing medical equipment and services through the application of the latest 3D technologies.

Our mission is to accelerate the implementation of new diagnostic and therapeutic approaches, improve prevention and medical personnel education through advanced 3D medical models and products.

Research

Mission

We develop and transfer innovations in cardiovascular medicine into real clinical practice through clinically grounded research, development of health technologies and digital solutions. Our mission is to accelerate the implementation of new diagnostic and therapeutic approaches, improve prevention and rehabilitation.

Vision

To become a leading regional partner for the development and commercialization of health technologies in cardiovascular medicine. We aim to market innovations with global potential from Serbia and strengthen scientific and technological capacity through Horizon Europe and international programs.

3D CARE Project

Innovative approach to education and preoperative preparation

Development of personalized medical models

Our 3D CARE project represents a revolutionary approach to education and preoperative patient preparation. By using the latest Stratasys J5 technology, we create personalized, high-fidelity medical models directly from patient DICOM scans.

Project Benefits:

  • ✓ More precise planning of surgical procedures
  • ✓ Reduced operation time
  • ✓ Improved intervention safety
  • ✓ Better outcomes for patients
  • ✓ Medical personnel education
  • ✓ Reduced chance of intraoperative complications

Application: Aneurysms, vascular malformations, complex cardiology cases and other anatomically challenging procedures.

Application Examples:

Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm - Personalized 3D models for planning endovascular interventions

Sinus of Valsalva Aneurysm - 3D reconstructions for complex cardiovascular procedures

Educational Models - Anatomically accurate models for training surgeons and medical personnel
